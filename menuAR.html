AR Restaurant Menu

Passionate about immersive technologies, I developed an augmented reality restaurant menu application. This application provides an experience where dishes are presented in AR, accompanied by their prices and associated ingredients.

The application is designed to be efficient, user-friendly, and intuitive.

Looking ahead:
- I plan to incorporate Ground Plan Detection into the project to enable AR dishes projection without the requirement for an image target.
- Additionally, I plan to develop a WebAR version to further extend the application's reach.
